The AWS Cloud Club - Adamson University (AWSCC - AdU) is a socio-civic, recognized student organization in Adamson University. AWS Cloud Clubs are student-led user groups globally recognized by the Amazon Web Services academic advocacy team. Our vision is to have a vibrant community that is for the students, by the students, wherein we aim to upskill and empower students through the use of AWS Technologies, regardless of their year and program.

As we begin the Academic Year 2025–2026 with newly appointed officers across organizations, we kindly seek your assistance in updating our internal partnership database. This will help streamline communication and coordination as we prepare for upcoming events and collaboration opportunities within the university.

Thank you very much for your time and support. Your cooperation is invaluable to fostering a more connected and collaborative student community.
